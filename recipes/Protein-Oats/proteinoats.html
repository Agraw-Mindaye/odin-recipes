Protein Oats

A quick and easy recipe for a healthy, balanced breakfast

This meal is a great source of protein and fiber

It can also be used as a preworkout meal

Ingredients

  • Rolled Oats
  • Milk
  • Protein Powder (any flavor)
  • Toppings (peanut butter, fruits, chocolate chips)

Instructions

  1. To a bowl, add 100g of rolled oats with 200ml of milk
  2. Microwave oats and milk for a minute and 30 seconds
  3. Take bowl out of microwave and and add a serving of protein powder (30g)
  4. Mix the powder with the oats and milk
  5. Serve with toppings of your choice
