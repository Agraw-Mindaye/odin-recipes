Protein Oats

A healthy breakfast or lunch idea

This meal is a great source of protein and carbohydrates

It can also be used as a pre/post workout meal

Ingredients

  • 3 Large Eggs
  • 2 tortillas
  • 1 serving (4 oz) of cooked chicken
  • Butter or oil
  • Seasonings (black pepper, salt)

Instructions

  1. Place a non-stick pan on medium heat and add oil or butter
  2. Crack 3 eggs in a large bowl and whisk while pan heats up
  3. Add eggs to pan and cook a thick omelette
  4. Split omelette into 2 pieces, one for each tortilla
  5. Add omelette pieces and cooked chicken to tortillas
  6. Wrap both tortillas and cook on pan for a minute on both sides
  7. Remove tortillas and serve with any sauce of your choice
